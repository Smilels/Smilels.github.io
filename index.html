Shuang Li

I'm Shuang, a roboticist by training. Currently I am working as a postdoc at the Munich Institute of Robotics and Machine Intelligence (MIRMI) in TUM, supervised by Prof. Haddadin. I got my Ph.D. at TAMS in Universität Hamburg in 03.2022. My diesseration focuses on robotic teleoperation, where I found out that vision-based perception provides an efficient way to teleoperate dexterous hand-arm systems. Welcome to my research blogs [here](blog) and feel free to discuss with me.
