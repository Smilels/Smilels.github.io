Lin Cong

I am a Research Assistant in Computer Science at TAMS. My primary research interests lie in the domains of AI and physics simulation. I started my academic career from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2014. Under the guidance of Prof. Jianwei Zhang, I earned my Ph.D. in 2022 from Universit of Hamburg. Currently, my research is centered on the integration of Physics Engines and Artificial Intelligence. Beyond my academic endeavors, I have a keen interest in crafting science and engineering projects in my spare time, which are displayed in the 'Leisure Pursuit' section.
