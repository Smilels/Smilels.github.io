Lin Cong

I am a Research Assistant in Computer Science at TAMS. My academic career began with a Bachelor's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2014, followed by postgraduate research at The State Key Laboratory of Robotics and Systems, focusing on Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. Under Prof. Jianwei Zhang, I earned my Ph.D. in 2022 from Universit of Hamburg. Besides research, I am enthusiastic about self-made science and engineering projects, showcased in the 'Leisure Pursuit' section.
